bench

bench is a catalog of public seating across Manhattan. Each entry records a single bench — its location, material, and orientation — as a point on a map. The project treats sitting as infrastructure: distributed, habitual, and largely undocumented.

method

Locations are found on foot and documented with coordinates (WGS 84), photographs, and field notes. Entries are assigned permanent numeric IDs and added to the archive in the order they are recorded.

data

Map tiles are provided by OpenStreetMap contributors via Leaflet. Coordinates use the WGS 84 datum. The catalog is updated as new benches are documented.
